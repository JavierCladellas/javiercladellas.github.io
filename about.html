ABOUT ME

I'm Javier Cladellas, 24 years old, born and raised in El Salvador.
I graduated highschool from the Lycée Français de San Salvador in 2016 and decided to move to Strasbourg, France, where I continued my studies in pure mathematics.
After getting my bachelor's degree, I returned to my home country for two years to start my professional life. During this time, I had the oportunity to work as a scientific researcher, as a team manager and developer, as a data scientist and as a university lecturer.
Currently living in Strasbourg, I remotely work as an actuarial consultant for Seguros Azul in El Salvador, while I finish my 2nd year of my master's degree in applied mathematics.
My degrees gave me the oportunity to improve my programming skills, as well as to learn data visualization and machine learning techniques. They have taught me how to permorm high-performance computing and how to solve complex optimization problems.
